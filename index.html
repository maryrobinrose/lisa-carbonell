Exceptional Craftsmanship

As a skilled woodworker and luthier, we specialize in high-end guitar building and repair since 2008.

In addition to guitar work, we also offer antique furniture refinishing and repair, utilizing traditional techniques and materials to preserve the beauty and integrity of these timeless pieces. Our dedication to quality and attention to detail ensures that every restoration is completed with the utmost care and precision.


As a luthier, Levi has a deep understanding of the acoustics of guitars, and can build or repair instruments that are visually stunning and sound beautiful. If you have an old guitar that needs some repair work, Levi can use his expertise to restore it to its former glory.

In addition to guitar work, Levi is also a skilled antique furniture restorer who can bring your old pieces back to life. He uses traditional techniques and materials to preserve the beauty and integrity of your furniture.

As an artist, Levi approaches every project with an eye for design and creativity. They use wood as a medium to create personalized and unique pieces that reflect your style and personality. And with a background in management, you can be sure that your project will be completed on time, within budget, and to the highest standards.

Above all, Levi is committed to providing friendly and personalized customer service. He takes the time to understand your needs and preferences, and work with you to bring your vision to life. Whether you are looking for a custom-built guitar or a beautifully restored piece of furniture, Levi has the skills and experience to deliver exceptional craftsmanship and a memorable customer experience.
